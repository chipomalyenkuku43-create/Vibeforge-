Welcome to Vibeforge

Vibeforge is a multi-feature creative mega app designed to empower creators, students, and researchers.

Access music creation, digital art, storytelling, AI-powered mathematics, digital marketplace, and AI fashion generation all in one unified platform.

📦 Status: Source code coming soon. Platform currently in development phase.

✨ Key Features

🎵

Music Engine

Beat generation in Amapiano, Rhumba, Gospel, Hip-Hop, Jazz, and more.

🎨

Art & Design

Digital art creation tools and design canvas for visual creators.

📖

Stories & Scripts

Story writing and screenplay creation with multilingual support.

🧮

AI Mathematics

AI-powered math problem solver with step-by-step explanations.

🛍️

Digital Marketplace

Buy and sell digital products and creative assets.

👗

Fashion Generator

AI-powered fashion design generator for unique concepts.
